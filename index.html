Skylight: Your One Stop Solution

About

Skylight is your one stop solution for your Internet of things connection. Everything from security to lighting to your fridge can be hooked into one streamlined system. By combining with compaitable systems, take control of your technology, and use it the way you want it to be used, by unlocking further features and greater control of your appliances.

Cross-compatibility

Skylight is now compatibale with many security services such as security cameras and alarms. They are also compatibale with programable LED lights.

ABOUT

Skylight and Skylight.net was started in 2021 as a Internet of Things (IOT) centred solution. There was a need identified for young families to better control and have access of their electronics and household appliances. What started as a simple program to have dynamic control of your appliances has grown into something much bigger.
